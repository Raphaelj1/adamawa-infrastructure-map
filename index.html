Transforming Data into Action – Adamawa Healthcare Infrastructure Map

Explore comprehensive healthcare data across northern Nigeria with our interactive map, designed to drive better decision-making and resource allocation.

Healthcare Infrastructure in Adamawa State – Operational Overview

Our project focuses on mapping healthcare facilities across Adamawa State, specifically highlighting primary healthcare facilities. The key infrastructure data includes:

  • Ownership

  • Facility Level

  • Operational Status

  • Operational: Facilities currently providing services.

  • Closed: Facilities that are no longer functioning.

  • Pending Construction: Facilities that are planned but not yet built.

  • Completed but Pending Operations: Facilities that are built but not yet open to the public.

Data Source

The data for this map was sourced from the Nigeria Health Facility Registry, ensuring accuracy and reliability in our representation of healthcare infrastructure.

Insights from the Map

Operational Facilities: These are the currently functional healthcare facilities that serve the public. They provide essential health services and are critical for daily operations.

Closed Facilities: These facilities represent gaps in healthcare service delivery. Reopening or replacing these could be key to improving health outcomes.

Pending Construction: Facilities in this category highlight areas where new infrastructure is planned but construction is yet to be completed. This data is crucial for understanding future healthcare expansion.

Completed but Pending Operations: These facilities are built but are not yet operational, signalling either administrative delays or resource allocation issues that need to be addressed.

Data Visualization

Our map is fully interactive, allowing users to:

  • Zoom in and out for a detailed view of healthcare facilities.

  • Filter by operational status, enabling a clear understanding of which facilities are functional and which need attention.

  • Click on specific facilities to get more detailed information, such as latitude, longitude, ownership, and status.

This infrastructure data will help decision-makers in planning healthcare development in Adamawa State. The visualization offers a user-friendly experience with distinct icons and color codes to differentiate between operational statuses, allowing for easy navigation and insight extraction.

Relevance for Decision-Making

The map highlights critical areas where healthcare infrastructure is either lacking or underutilized. Decision-makers can use this information to prioritize investments in closed and pending healthcare facilities to improve overall healthcare delivery in Adamawa State. By focusing on operational gaps, this project provides actionable insights for strategic healthcare planning.
